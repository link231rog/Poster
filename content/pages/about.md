I work at the boundary between research and engineering.

The projects I enjoy most are small systems with clear interfaces: tools that reduce friction, sites that age well, and workflows that stay understandable after the first release.

Right now I care about three things: building with deliberate constraints, making technical work easier to maintain, and turning rough ideas into stable public artifacts.
